Dated Athlete Settings
Why Dated Athlete Settings? The evolution of your physiological attributes over time (heart rate, weight, power, pace, ...) has to be supported because activities' stats including stress scores are functions of these attributes. After configuration by yourself, this feature will provide matching athlete's attributes for a given activity date to calculate stats with actual day's attributes. When enabled, Dated Athlete Settings will be used by all Elevate features which might need it (e.g. fitness trend).
warning   Functional Thresholds! Please read carefully!
If you don't provide your Cycling, Running, Swim Functional Thresholds (FTPs) in the below settings, you will get EMPTY STRESS SCORES for activities performed without a heart rate monitor or a real power meter. This will result in a FLAT FITNESS TREND. To see how-to configure your various FTPs, just add or edit dated athlete settings.
If you are not aware of your past physiological attributes on the first configuration/sync, simply enter some dated athlete settings with rough values and sync! This will allow you to grab the required "static" stats on some "revealing" synced activities! In activities section and for every 4-6 weeks: find activities where you hit your thresholds. Check Max hr,Cycling best 20min power (= ~Cycling FTP), Running best 20min pace (= ~Running FTP), Swim best 20min pace (= ~Swim FTP), ... and create/update dated athlete settings with corresponding values! You will be prompted to recalculate your activities. Do it! All your stats, stress scores, graphs will be updated. Repeat this history analysis & recalculation as much as needed.
